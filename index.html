About Katherine

Jack-of-all-trades, Speedy Gonzalez, multihyphenate, pink-haired girl - these are all names I've heard about myself from my superiors and colleagues in my 10 years of design experience. Naturally, I'm a helper, and I aim to create things that make someone's life a little simpler. Combine that with my business acumen, and you'll find I get things done and I get results. I have an understanding of what it takes to get something made and am incredibly detailed in my time-management and control of scope creep.

My client list is expansive and extends to almost every industry. From working with small-town politicians to large Fortune 500 companies, I have insight into how organizations and people work differently, and how to find a solution in a plethora of situations.

Feel free to get in touch with me about opportunities in design and front-development.
