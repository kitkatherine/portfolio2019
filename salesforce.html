Salesforce

Trailblazer's Guide to Apps

A carefully crafted feature of most important case studies sprinkled with fun custom animations and characters.

Launch Project

BACKGROUND

Salesforce came to use with a long, detailed PDF. With a conference upcoming, they wanted something to encourage their customers to download and read the PDF, while also embracing their brand and characters. With a fast-approaching deadline, they also needed a quick turnaround.

GOAL

This interactive eBook would help to gather leads through web traffic and encourage interaction with Salesforce, while also providing Salesforce with user data on interactions with the content inside of the eBook to inform further interactive and static content pieces.

TIMELINE

Project was completed over three months from concept to final launch.

Strategy

The primary strategy was focused around mobile users and scaling back the content to be digestible on any device. Coupling this with a general eBook structure from the ion Quick Start cloud allowed the Salesforce team to use the vast majority of their scope focused on customizedanimations done with CSS. A total of five apps were chosen based on industries with the highest amount of Salesforce clients.

Challenges

After the prototyping stage, the client struggled with their brand and our design not aligning as expected. I was brought into the project at the design phase to help speak with the client and re-align the vision on this project. Securing additional funding for the project from the client, I was able to refocus the project and bring to life the final design as it stands today.
